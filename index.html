Who is Sean Combs?

Sean Love Combs also known as Puff Daddy, P.Diddy, Diddy, Puffy or Love, is an American rapper, songwriter, and entrepreneur.

  • Birthday: November,4 1969
  • City: Harlem, New York, NY
  • Age: 52
  • Children: 6
  • Networth: $930 Million US Dollars
  • Business: Sean John
  • Men perfume "I AM KING"
  • Major stake in Revolt.tv

As reported by REVOLT, Diddy sold a 90 percent majority stake in Sean John – which has been modeled by the likes of Nelly, T.I., Mariah Carey, Mary J. Blige and more – to Global Brands Group back in 2016. This July, the company’s U.S. arm filed for Chapter 11 bankruptcy in New York and began selling off its key assets. Diddy initially offered a $3.3 million bid for Sean John, but ended up paying more than double the amount in cash to reclaim the brand.
